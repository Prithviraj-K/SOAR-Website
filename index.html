ABOUT US

SOAR (Suddenly Opportunities are Reachable) is a leadership conference for students in grades seven and eight attending a middle school in the Peel District School Board. This one-day event is an amazing opportunity to meet new people, build leadership skills, and learn more about high school life and why it’s not so scary! Throughout the day delegates will work side by side with experienced high school mentors in completing specifically designed activities that mirror upcoming challenges in their lives. Delegates will also have the opportunity to ask our mentors any personal questions pertaining to our conference aims.
